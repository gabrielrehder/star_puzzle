★ Star Battle
A little side project — I wanted to build a puzzle game for my dad to enjoy on his phone.
Star Battle is a logic puzzle: place exactly 2 stars in every row, every column, and every coloured region. Stars may not touch each other, not even diagonally.
How to play
Open the link, pick a difficulty, and tap cells to place stars. Tap again to place a dot marker, tap once more to clear. The puzzle is solved when all 20 stars are placed correctly.
Features

Four difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert
Puzzles are generated fresh every time — no two are the same
Tracks your solve rate per difficulty
Skip a puzzle and come back to it later
Works offline after the first visit
Installable as a home screen app on iPhone and Android

Built with
A single self-contained HTML file — no frameworks, no dependencies, no server logic. Just vanilla JavaScript and a canvas renderer.
